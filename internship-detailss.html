Senior Product Designer

We are looking for a talented & creative artist with an advanced level of English to design UI/UX interfaces for web and mobile apps.

Department
Design
Location

Salary

UI/UX Design Intern

Your mission

You’ll be collaborating with other team members in a cross-functional product team, to build spending experiences for personal and business customers.

Here’s how you’ll be contributing

  • You’ll get to know customers, building empathy and a deep understanding of their needs.
  • You’ll use Product Design skills to discover, design and deliver simple experiences users love.
  • You’ll influence your team, increasing customer-centeredness, creativity and design quality.

About you

  • You’ve got a portfolio of well-crafted work that solves real problems for real people.
  • You’re a natural collaborator, designing in the open and leveraging other functions’ expertise.
  • You practice evidence-based design and can rationalize decisions clearly and credibly.
